Internships

Internships are provided of 1-2 months approximately in both summer and winter.

We have designed our internship program to emphasize on advanced technologies in both Hardware and Software. This program enables the student to understand and work on different domains using industry-oriented technologies and practices. The student is first exposed to the new state-of-art areas and finally assigned with mini-projects to help them gain practical knowledge of the particular domain. The student is also encouraged to work on their own ideas and collaborate with our team of professionals for feasible execution. We provide internship during summer May-July and winter November-January.
